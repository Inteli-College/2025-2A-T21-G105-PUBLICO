# Part 1 — Executive Overview and Business Canvas  

## 1.1 Executive Summary  

**RenovAI** is an intelligent renovation assistant that helps users plan and execute home improvement projects through a conversational interface powered by **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **immersive visualization (AR/VR)**.  

By translating user preferences — such as room type, dimensions, aesthetic style, and budget — into a **visual layout** and an automatically generated **smart shopping cart**, RenovAI connects clients with partner retailers, bridging the digital and physical experiences of home design.  

At the final stage, users can generate a **token or QR code** to visit a physical store, where the app’s digital plan is used to complete purchases or schedule delivery. This seamless **O2O (online-to-offline)** model ensures that retailers capture qualified leads while users gain confidence, efficiency, and personalization in their renovation process.  

---

## 1.2 Problem Statement  

Most people who wish to renovate or furnish a room **don’t know where to begin**. Planning, selecting furniture, defining styles, and ensuring compatibility between items are complex and time-consuming tasks.  

Hiring architects or interior designers is often **financially inaccessible**, especially for middle-income families. As a result, consumers rely on guesswork, fragmented online searches, and physical visits to multiple stores — leading to frustration, delays, and wasted resources.  

The market lacks a **simple, affordable, and guided digital experience** that can assist users from initial concept to purchase, democratizing access to professional-quality results.

---

## 1.3 Solution Overview  

RenovAI provides a **digital ecosystem** where users can:  
- Describe their space and goals in natural language.  
- Receive **personalized product recommendations** powered by AI.  
- **Visualize furniture and color schemes** in their actual environment via AR.  
- Save preferred combinations and generate **purchase lists or store tokens**.  

For retailers, RenovAI acts as a **lead-generation and conversion tool**, integrating online catalogs with in-store experiences to drive measurable sales and reduce product return rates.  

---

## 1.4 Objectives  

RenovAI pursues five strategic objectives:  

1. **Democratize intelligent renovations**  
   - Provide accessible, AI-driven planning tools to replace part of the cost and complexity of hiring professionals.  
2. **Deliver end-to-end customer support**  
   - Guide users from project conception to product purchase with an intuitive and human-centered experience.  
3. **Increase retailer conversion rates**  
   - Channel qualified customers directly to partner stores through integrated digital-to-physical journeys.  
4. **Develop an intelligent, interactive catalog**  
   - Maintain dynamic product databases enriched by AI-based compatibility and visualization features.  
5. **Build a scalable digital ecosystem**  
   - Support future integrations with marketplaces, professionals, and AR-based shopping experiences.  

---

RenovAI simplifies and humanizes the renovation process by turning complex design and purchasing decisions into a guided, visual conversation. Users can experiment with layouts, styles, and color palettes before spending any money, saving time, effort, and reducing anxiety.  

**For retailers:**  
RenovAI delivers high-intent leads, connects digital engagement to in-store sales, and enables data-driven insights on consumer preferences.  

**For professionals (designers, architects):**  
It offers a channel for exposure, collaboration, and potential client acquisition through a shared ecosystem.

# Part 2 — Product Description and Development Roadmap  

## 2.1 Product Overview  

**RenovAI** is a digital platform that combines **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **Augmented Reality (AR)** to guide users through the entire renovation journey — from initial planning to product purchase.  

The system captures information such as the type of room, dimensions, preferred styles, and budget, then generates:  
1. A **visual layout** (2D/3D) with suggested furniture, color palettes, and finishes.  
2. A **smart shopping cart** containing compatible products sourced from partner retailers.  
3. A **token/QR code** to connect the user’s project with a physical store, enabling in-person visits or checkout.  

The platform integrates **machine learning**, **image generation**, and **immersive visualization**, offering both consumers and retailers a simple yet powerful digital experience.  

---

## 2.2 Scope of Deliverables  

The following deliverables constitute the RenovAI MVP and its immediate evolution phases:  

- **Conversational AI interface** for user understanding and preference extraction.  
- **Recommendation engine** suggesting products and materials based on user inputs.  
- **Augmented Reality visualization module** for real-scale object placement and color testing.  
- **Product catalog database** with structured information (dimensions, materials, price, images, and 3D assets).  
- **Smart cart and token system** linking digital projects to physical retail partners.  
- **Retailer dashboard (B2B)** for catalog management and viewing project leads.  
- **Web and mobile integration**, supporting iOS, Android, and responsive PWA access.  

---

## 2.3 System Architecture Overview  

RenovAI adopts a **modular and scalable architecture**:  

- **Frontend (React / Next.js)** — user interface, onboarding forms, AR visualization, and chat-based assistant.  
- **Backend (Python / FastAPI)** — orchestration of AI agents, product catalog management, and API endpoints.  
- **Database (PostgreSQL)** — storage of user sessions, preferences, and partner product data.  
- **AI Layer (LangChain + Gemini API)** — conversation management, natural language understanding, and product-matching logic.  
- **AR/VR Layer (Unity + AR Foundation)** — native AR visualization for mobile MVP; VR showrooms in selected partner stores.  
- **Cloud Infrastructure (AWS / GCP)** — hosting, model storage (3D assets), and analytics pipeline for KPIs.  

The architecture supports **future extensions**, such as credit calculators, FGTS renovation guidance, and designer-style agents.

# Part 3 — Market Research Summary  

## 3.1 Research Objective  

The RenovAI market research aimed to identify and quantify the main **pain points, behaviors, and expectations** of Brazilian consumers regarding **home renovations and interior design**.  
The study sought to validate the **demand** for an accessible, AI-driven renovation platform and to map the **competitive landscape** of digital tools currently serving the market.

---

## 3.2 Methodology  

- **Primary research:** proprietary online survey with **117 respondents** (Feb/2025), targeting Classes A, B, and upper C in Brazil’s **Southeast and South regions**.  
- **Secondary research:** industry reports from **E-commerce Brasil (2024)**, **CETIC.br / TIC Domicílios 2023**, and **MDPI (2024)** on AR adoption in furniture retail.  
- **Qualitative analysis:** interviews with consumers and retail professionals to explore perceptions of technology and pain points in renovation planning.  

---

## 3.3 Key Findings — Consumer Behavior and Pain Points  

### 1. **Planning Difficulties and Overload**  
- **74%** of respondents said they *“don’t know where to start”* when planning a renovation.  
- **61%** fear **budget overruns** and lack of transparency in service pricing.  
- **52%** report **difficulty visualizing** the final result before purchasing materials or furniture.  

### 2. **Trust and Decision-Making**  
- **68%** rely mainly on **friends’ or relatives’ recommendations** to hire professionals.  
- Only **19%** trust existing digital marketplaces or renovation platforms.  
- **84%** stated that **“seeing how the environment would look before buying”** would strongly influence their purchase decision.  

### 3. **Digital Engagement Patterns**  
- **85%** access the internet primarily through **smartphones**, confirming the importance of a **mobile-first strategy**.  
- **67%** use **Pinterest or Instagram** to find inspiration but struggle to translate that into actionable renovation plans.  
- Consumers favor **visual, interactive, and guided experiences** rather than text-heavy or catalog-only tools.

# Part 4 — Technology and Feasibility Summary  

## 4.1 Purpose of the Technical Study  

The technical feasibility analysis aimed to determine the maturity, cost, and scalability of immersive and intelligent technologies applicable to RenovAI.  
The focus was to validate that Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and Artificial Intelligence (AI) could be implemented with realistic budgets, reliable user experience, and short-term deployability in the Brazilian market.

---

## 4.2 Technology Overview  

RenovAI integrates three main technological pillars:  

**Artificial Intelligence (AI)** – Responsible for understanding user intent, extracting structured data from conversations, and recommending products consistent with preferences and constraints. The system uses natural language processing through the Gemini API and orchestration via LangChain, allowing contextual responses and data persistence across sessions.  

**Augmented Reality (AR)** – Enables visualization of furniture and finishes in real scale using markerless tracking. It supports plane detection, lighting estimation, and occlusion effects that merge digital objects with real environments. The AR module is developed with Unity and AR Foundation, ensuring compatibility with both Android (ARCore) and iOS (ARKit).  

**Virtual Reality (VR)** – Used in physical stores and showrooms as a demonstration tool. Through VR headsets such as Meta Quest 3, users can walk through a simulated environment that mirrors their renovation plan, enhancing trust and engagement during purchase decisions.  

---

## 4.3 Recommended Technical Stack  

| Layer | Technology | Purpose |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Frontend** | React / Next.js | Web interface, onboarding forms, integration with chat and catalog |
| **Backend** | Python / FastAPI | Orchestration of AI agents, data processing, API routes |
| **Database** | PostgreSQL | Structured storage of users, products, and sessions |
| **AI Orchestration** | LangChain + Gemini API | Conversation management and product recommendation |
| **AR/VR Engine** | Unity + AR Foundation | Cross-platform immersive visualization |
| **3D File Formats** | glTF 2.0 / GLB and USDZ | Efficient, PBR-compatible model delivery |
| **Cloud Infrastructure** | AWS or GCP | Storage, analytics, and content delivery network (CDN) for 3D assets |

This stack balances performance, cost, and accessibility. It leverages existing developer ecosystems and open standards, minimizing the need for proprietary or expensive technologies.

---

## 4.4 Technological Maturity  

The study confirmed that AR and AI technologies have reached commercial maturity suitable for RenovAI’s MVP.  
Apple’s ARKit and Google’s ARCore support over 1.2 billion devices globally, including the majority of smartphones used in Brazil. Markerless AR, plane detection, and lighting estimation have become standard features since 2022, ensuring stability for mass deployment.  

Similarly, AI frameworks such as LangChain and Gemini API offer robust capabilities for semantic understanding and contextual recommendation without the need for heavy on-device computation, allowing scalable cloud integration.

---

## 4.5 Architecture and Integration Approach  

RenovAI follows a modular architecture to simplify development and ensure long-term scalability.  

1. **Frontend Layer** – Collects user data through guided questions and visual inputs.  
2. **AI Layer** – Processes user messages to extract room type, measurements, style, and budget.  
3. **Recommendation Engine** – Matches structured inputs with catalog metadata and returns compatible products.  
4. **Visualization Layer** – Displays results through 2D mockups or real-scale AR objects, using Unity’s rendering pipeline.  
5. **Data Persistence and Feedback Loop** – Stores user sessions, monitors behavior, and refines suggestions over time.  

The modular approach allows future upgrades, such as designer-style agents, gamification features, and cost calculators, without refactoring the system’s core.

---

---

## 4.7 Key Risks and Mitigation  

| Risk | Description | Mitigation Strategy |
|-------|--------------|---------------------|
| **User adoption** | Low familiarity with AR tools among non-technical users | Simplified onboarding, visual tutorials, mobile-first UX |
| **Retailer participation** | Hesitation to share product data or commissions | Clear partnership contracts and pilot incentives |
| **3D content availability** | Limited number of realistic models at launch | Gradual catalog expansion, focusing on best-selling products |
| **Performance constraints** | Mid-range smartphones with lower processing power | Texture compression (KTX2), LOD optimization, fallback to 3D-only view |
| **Infrastructure cost** | Cloud and storage expenses with catalog growth | Adaptive caching, serverless APIs, efficient CDN use |

The project maintains a manageable risk profile, with no single dependency capable of halting implementation.  

---

## 4.8 Legal, Privacy, and Accessibility Considerations  

The system complies with **Brazil’s LGPD (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados)**, applying principles of data minimization and transparency. Camera input is processed locally by ARCore or ARKit without storing image frames. Optional features, such as before-and-after photos or room scans, require explicit user consent.  

Accessibility follows **WCAG 2.2 Level AA** guidelines. Touch targets, text contrast, and simplified navigation are prioritized to serve a wide range of users, including those with limited digital literacy.  

---

## 4.9 Technical Feasibility Conclusion  

The RenovAI MVP is fully feasible with existing technology and can be built with current resources available in Brazil.  
AR and AI platforms are stable, accessible, and cost-effective, while 3D modeling and cloud services are within reach for early-stage ventures.  
The project’s scalability depends primarily on content expansion and partnership growth rather than technical barriers.  

The combination of robust architecture, modular design, and commercially mature technologies ensures that RenovAI can be deployed within **9 to 12 months** and progressively expanded into a national platform for intelligent home renovation.

## 5.3 Revenue Model Details  

**1. Sales Commissions (O2O Model)**  
RenovAI tracks the customer journey from digital interaction to in-store purchase through unique tokens or QR codes. When a sale originates from the app, a commission (typically 5–10%) is charged to the retailer.  

**2. Premium B2B Subscriptions**  
Retailers and brands can subscribe to advanced features such as priority placement in search results, detailed analytics dashboards, and customer behavior reports.  

**3. Advertising and Featured Listings**  
Partner brands may pay for banner placements or “recommended products” positions within the platform’s catalog, similar to marketplace sponsorships.  

**4. Consulting and Data Insights**  
Aggregated data on design trends, product preferences, and consumer behavior can be monetized through consulting partnerships and annual market reports.  

**5. Consumer Upsell Options (Freemium Tier)**  
Basic features remain free for all users. Future paid options may include premium visualization modes, style-matching tools, and professional consultations with certified designers.
